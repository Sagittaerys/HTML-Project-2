Delicious Bites Menu

Appetizers

  • Bruschetta

    An Italian appetizer consisting of grilled bread topped with diced tomatoes, garlic, basil, and olive oil.

  • Spring Rolls

    A light and crispy appetizer filled with vegetables, sometimes meat, wrapped in thin dough and fried or served fresh.

  • Stuffed Mushrooms

    Mushroom caps filled with a mixture of cheese, garlic, herbs, and sometimes sausage or breadcrumbs, then baked until golden.

  • Caprese Skewers

    Mini skewers with fresh mozzarella, cherry tomatoes, and basil, drizzled with balsamic glaze for a refreshing bite.

Main Courses

  • Grilled Salmon

    A healthy dish featuring salmon fillets seasoned with herbs and lemon, grilled to perfection for a smoky flavor.

  • Chicken Alfredo Pasta

    A creamy Italian pasta dish with fettuccine noodles, grilled chicken, and a rich Alfredo sauce made from butter, cream, and Parmesan cheese.

  • Beef Steak with Mashed Potatoes

    A juicy, pan-seared or grilled beef steak served with creamy mashed potatoes and a side of vegetables.

  • Jollof Rice with Grilled Chicken

    A West African classic, this dish consists of rice cooked in a flavorful tomato-based sauce, served with spicy grilled chicken.

Desserts

  • Chocolate Lava Cake

    A rich and decadent chocolate cake with a gooey, molten chocolate center, often served with ice cream.

  • Tiramisu

    A classic Italian dessert made with layers of coffee-soaked ladyfingers, mascarpone cheese, cocoa powder, and a hint of liqueur.

  • Cheesecake

    A creamy and smooth dessert with a buttery graham cracker crust, available in flavors like classic New York-style, strawberry, or chocolate.

  • Puff Puff

    A popular West African deep-fried dough ball, slightly sweet, fluffy on the inside, and crispy on the outside, often enjoyed as a snack or dessert.
